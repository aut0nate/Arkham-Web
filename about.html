About Arkham

A London AI and Automation Studio Built for Practical Change.

Founded in London in 2015, Arkham helps teams turn AI, automation, and cloud capability into working software that removes friction from real operations.

Overview

Practical AI Systems for Teams That Need Work to Move.

Arkham is a London-based AI and automation studio working with organisations that want useful technology rather than abstract transformation theatre. Since 2015, the studio has focused on designing, building, and improving tools that help teams make decisions faster, automate repetitive work, and connect fragmented systems into clearer operating flows.

The work sits where product design, automation engineering, cloud delivery, and AI implementation meet. That means Arkham does not treat AI as a standalone feature. It is shaped into applications, dashboards, agents, workflows, and managed services that fit the way people already work.

Arkham's style is direct and delivery-led: understand the process, build a focused version, test it with real users, then harden it for the environment it must run in. The result is software that is easier to operate, easier to explain, and easier to keep improving.

Mission

Make advanced technology useful, maintainable, and measurable.

Arkham's mission is to help organisations apply AI and automation to the work that matters most: reducing manual effort, improving decisions, strengthening controls, and giving teams dependable tools they can trust day to day.

Vision

A future where every team can operate with intelligent support built around their work.

Arkham's vision is for AI and automation to become practical infrastructure for modern organisations: visible, governed, secure, and designed around people rather than replacing their judgement.
